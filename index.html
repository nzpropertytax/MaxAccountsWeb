About Us

Christchurch-based accounting and business services.

Max Accountancy was founded by Craig and Jenny - two professionals from different backgrounds who share a belief in each other's skills and a passion for enabling businesses to succeed. Based in Christchurch, New Zealand, we pride ourselves on delivering clear, practical advice and personalised customer service, all at affordable prices.

We believe the foundation of a successful business is having robust systems and processes in place, enabling efficiency and reliability. This is what makes a business competitive. With over 20 years' experience in accounting, ERP systems, and business process improvement, we help our clients not only meet compliance requirements but can also streamline operations, improve efficiency, and plan for growth.

Accounting is not just about your Tax obligations and compliance requirements, we here at Max Accounting understand that. We aim to help your business succeed by providing timely and useful financial advice and tax planning alongside the assisting in the activation of operation systems to help with business processes such as client and stock management.

Max Accounting offers more than just accounting. We can assist with a variety of touchpoints for your business from Web Design, set up and support of your Accounting Systems (MYOB, XERO, etc.) as well as traditional Tax Filing and Financial advice. We're your partner in building a stronger, more sustainable business.
